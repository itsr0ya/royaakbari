NaviCare mobile app

Figma prototype

Overview

NaviCare addresses the challenges vulnerable individuals face when accessing essential services like housing, legal aid, and financial support. Information is often scattered and difficult to navigate, especially for those facing language barriers, low digital literacy, and complex application processes.

This project is informed by both research and my experience at MultiLink Community Services, where I’ve seen how confusing these systems can be for culturally and linguistically diverse clients, highlighting the need for a clearer and more accessible solution.

Target Audience

The primary audience are newly arrived refugees and migrants, people experiencing homelessness, and individuals facing financial stress.

Tools and Technologies

Figma was used throughout the design process, from early sketching and low-fidelity wireframes to developing high-fidelity prototypes. It supported the exploration of layout ideas, user flows, and iterative refinements, allowing designs to evolve based on feedback and testing.
